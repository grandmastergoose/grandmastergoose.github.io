About this Site

The purpose of this site is to highlight some key tourist spots for residents or visitors that might have interest in. Los Angeles is full of great restaurants with a variety of new foods to try some being familiar and some new. It is also home to many sports with diverse teams to match its fans. There are provided links for site viewers that may have further interest in the highlights provided. There is also a google search bar to yelp so that users can read reviews they might want to visit.
